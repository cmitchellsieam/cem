Craig E. Mitchell, EAMP, PhD

Chinese Herbal Medicine, Acupuncture, Tuina

Practice

My practice involves the use of acupuncture, tui-na (Chinese medical massage), Chinese herbal medicine, lifestyle advice, and dietary recommendations. I work in a collaborative process with my patients and their other healthcare providers, when needed, to develop a treatment plan that meets short and long-term health goals.

Academic

I graduated from the American College of Traditional Chinese Medicine (SF, CA) in 1993. Since 1993, I have been seeing patients, teaching, and writing books, including On Cold Damage, a translation with commentary of the Shang Han Lun. Currently, I teach and serve as Executive Director at the Seattle Institute of East Asian Medicine.
